Customer Support

How do I connect?
Open BentoDeck on Mac and tap CONNECT to show a 4‑digit PIN. On iPhone, open BentoDeck and enter the PIN. Keep both devices on the same Wi‑Fi / local network when possible.
Does BentoDeck use the cloud?
No. BentoDeck connects directly to your Mac over your local network. We do not run servers for your content.
How do I restore a purchase?
Open the purchase panel and tap RESTORE PURCHASES.
