Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2026

BentoDeck is built to be local‑first. Our goal is to let you control your Mac from iPhone without creating an account and without sending your content to any BentoDeck servers.

1. Data We Collect

BentoDeck does not collect, store, or sell your personal data, your app list, or your control actions for advertising.

2. Local Network Connection

BentoDeck uses your local network to discover and connect to your Mac. Connection happens directly between your devices.

  • Pairing uses a 4‑digit PIN shown on your Mac.
  • After pairing, BentoDeck may store local pairing information on your devices to make reconnecting faster.

3. On‑Device Storage

BentoDeck stores settings (such as your grid layout and pairing info) locally on your devices. We do not provide a cloud sync service.

4. Third‑Party Analytics

BentoDeck does not include third‑party analytics SDKs designed to track you across apps and websites.
